## Market Interpretation

The market is converging around agent orchestration, workflow execution, tools, memory, human-in-the-loop flows, and observability. Entangle's distinct angle is graph-native organizational modeling and artifact-first handoff. LatticeOps should emphasize that difference rather than compete as another general chain or workflow framework.

## Research Limitation

The external landscape changes quickly. Vendor capabilities, pricing, and product names should be rechecked before using this wiki for investor materials, competitive sales enablement, or public positioning.
